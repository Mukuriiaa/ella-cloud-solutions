Our Story
Who We Are

Ella Cloud Solutions Limited is a cutting edge ICT consulting firm offering services and sourcing products for a broad range of computer users in various sectors across Kenya and beyond.

We provide consultancy services for customized and general applications in Computer Systems Design and Installation, Communications and Networking, Systems Management and Information Planning and Telecommunications.

Our core team brings over 35 years of combined experience in the IT industry, including private user consultancy and training. We are supported by qualified professional administrators with sound financial and business management experience at both domestic and international levels.

We are affiliated with various partners located around the world — leaders in their respective fields commanding a good market share and understanding of their respective product lines.
