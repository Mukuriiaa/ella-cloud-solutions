Satellite Services
High Performance Satellite Internet

We offer a stable and reliable internet connection by satellite across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia and the entire African continent — perfect for businesses in areas with limited terrestrial connectivity.

We operate on the NSS-12 satellite using the latest DVB-S2 ACM technology — providing significantly higher data flow in both directions with added security and robustness even in adverse weather conditions.

DVB-S2 — New modulation technology for stronger satellite links
ACM Rx/Tx — Adaptive Coding for higher data flow both ways
Shared & dedicated access options available
24/7 online monitoring and management portal
Subscriptions with minimum guaranteed speeds (CIR)
Volume based refill options available
